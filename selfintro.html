ABOUT
Hello my name is Joanna Bowman.I am currently a junior enrolled full-time in the Bachelors of Interactive and Graphic Design program at Millersville University. Some of my favorite design classes I have taken include: Material Design, Illustration, and Motion Design. Outside of Millersville I am a part time graphic designer for Utz Quality Foods where monthly I design advertisements, promotions, coupons, and videos for various platforms.

Although most of the professional work I do is digital, I love to paint and draw in my free time. I hold a seat on the executive board of the Millersville Chapter of the National Society of Leadership and Success where we strive to branch out, network, and make connections.

To make some extra money and get my work out there I sell some of my designs on Redbubble and Society6. If you would like to know more about me or see some more of my work I have links to my Design Instagram, Redbubble, and Society6.
